Pai Vista Convention Hall, KR Road

Discover the perfect venue for extraordinary occasions at Pai Vista Convention Hall, KR Road. With plush interiors, excellent vegetarian cuisines and professional services, we provide a distinct setting for large weddings, receptions and social gatherings.With world-class facilities, professional staff and personalised services, we create memorable and successful occasions for all your social gatherings and business events.

welcome to Pai Vista Convention Hall, KR Road, where we offer an air-conditioned and plush venue for extraordinary occasions. Whether you're planning a grand wedding, a joyous birthday celebration or any other social gathering, our facility provides a distinct and versatile space to make your event truly memorable.

Key features of our convention hall include:

  • Two separate banquet halls with change rooms and a separate dining facility
  • Elevator facility for easy accessibility
  • Excellent vegetarian cuisines tailored to your preference
  • Buffets and cocktails for a delightful dining experience
  • Traditional plantain leaf service for a touch of cultural charm
  • Halls/venues that can be operated simultaneously and independently
  • State-of-the-art audio-visual equipment for enhanced presentations
  • Professional staff offering efficient and attentive service

We take pride in delivering exceptional culinary experiences that cater to the most discerning gourmets. Our dedicated team ensures that every aspect, from food to decor and facilities, is tailored to meet your specific requirements.
