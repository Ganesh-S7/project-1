Banquet Hall 3

Maple Hall at Hotel Pai Vista, K.R. Road, Bangalore is a versatile and well-furnished event venue in Bangalore suitable for both corporate and social gatherings. With air-conditioning for guest comfort, it offers a productive meeting environment complete with amenities like bottled water, cookies, tea, coffee, writing pads and pens. Additionally, valet parking is available and the hall can be transformed into a banquet space with delicious catered meals from expert chefs. The maximum capacities of Maple Hall as per the following seating arrangements are:

  • Theatre layout - 150
  • U-shaped layout - 50
  • Classroom layout - 65
  • Cluster Layout - 65

Address

Pai Vista Convention Hall, KR Road, Opp to Sevakshetra Hospital, Near Monotype, Banashankari 2nd Stage, Bangalore - 560070.
