Banquet Hall 1

Banquet Hall 2 at Hotel Pai Vista, KR Road, Bangalore offers a versatile space suitable for any type of event. With a focus on providing a conducive environment for productive meetings, this hall is equipped with amenities such as complimentary bottled water, cookies, tea, coffee, writing pads and pens. Guests can also take advantage of valet parking services. The maximum capacities of Banquet Hall 3 as per the following seating arrangements are:

  • Theatre layout - 300 to 350
  • Classroom layout - 180
  • Floating crowd - 500 to 600
  • Cluster layout - 150

Address

Pai Vista Convention Hall, KR Road, Opp to Sevakshetra Hospital, Near Monotype, Banashankari 2nd Stage, Bangalore - 560070.
